What We Do

Expert
Conservation
Services

From field assessments to policy frameworks, we deliver end-to-end solutions rooted in ecological science and community partnership.

01

🦁

Wildlife Corridor Planning

Designing functional landscape linkages that allow wildlife to move freely across fragmented habitats, reducing isolation and genetic bottlenecks.

02

🌿

Biodiversity Assessments

Rigorous field surveys and remote-sensing analysis to establish baseline biodiversity inventories and track ecosystem health over time.

03

🌱

Habitat Restoration

Evidence-based native-species planting programmes and invasive removal strategies that rebuild degraded ecosystems from the ground up.

04

📋

Conservation Policy & Strategy

Translating ecological science into actionable government policy, land-use frameworks, and conservancy management plans.

05

🔭

Species Monitoring

Camera-trap networks, acoustic monitoring, and DNA sampling programmes to track threatened species populations with precision.

06

🤝

Community Conservation

Structuring community conservancy models that align local livelihoods with long-term wildlife protection and benefit-sharing outcomes.

Who We Are

Science,
Field Experience
& Purpose

Verdant was founded by a team of wildlife ecologists, conservation biologists, and policy specialists who have spent decades working in some of Africa and Asia's most biodiverse landscapes.

We don't offer off-the-shelf solutions. Every engagement starts in the field — understanding the land, the species, and the people who share it.
